About DBIT

Don Bosco Institute of Technology aims to be Bangalore's leading engineering college, committed to nurturing and transforming talent through quality, value-based education. Our vision is to become a premier center of excellence in technology and management. We offer cutting-edge facilities that support advanced learning and strive to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed for a successful future.

We are a top engineering college in Bengaluru, The premier institute, which is affiliated to VTU, Belagavi, enriches the landscape of technical education by offering UG (BE) Programmes in Computer Science, Information Science, Electronics and Communication, Electrical and Electronics, Telecommunication, Mechanical and Civil Engineering. It also offers an MBA Programme.

Why Engineering at DBIT

Top-Ranked Excellence

DBIT is ranked #64 by The Times of India, known for its academic rigor, experienced faculty, and exceptional engineering outcomes.

Industry Aligned Curriculum

Our curriculum is crafted with direct input from industry leaders, ensuring students gain relevant skills aligned with today’s tech-driven world.

Innovation Labs for experiential Learning

DBIT offers cutting-edge labs that promote hands-on learning, innovation, and research — empowering students to build, test, and solve real-world challenges.

Rewarding Scholarship Opportunity

We offer merit-based and need-based scholarships that encourage bright minds to pursue engineering careers without financial constraints.

Strong Industry Collaborations

With 100+ industry tie-ups, DBIT partners with companies like Infosys, Wipro, TCS, IBM to ensure students are industry-ready.

Entrepreneurial Empowerment
